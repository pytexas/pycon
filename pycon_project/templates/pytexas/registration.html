PyTexas 2011 Registration

PyTexas is free and open to the public. Please note that data posted to this survey will be made public, except for email addresses!

If you're interested in attending PyTexas please do register, even if you cannot make it this year. This helps let us know who in the community to contact in planning the schedule for next year.

If you think you might need to change your answers later, you can bookmark the link "Edit your response" after you press Submit. Returning to that link gives you a chance to modify the answers you previously made.

For those curious about how the data such as email addresses will be collected, stored, and handled, please see About This Site.
